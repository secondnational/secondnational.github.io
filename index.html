About Us

A 100% Omani company based in Muscat, Sultanate of Oman, operating in Oil & Gas sectors and transportation domains. It continues to expand and play a major role in commercializing new business ideas to contribute to the development of the national and regional economy.

In order to serve the customers, The Second National LLC is investing in its employees, internal processes, tools, and business systems. We are strengthening our existing core competencies as well as focus on the development of new capabilities to meet increasing requirements.

The Second National was established in the year 2006, and progressively has been mounting to a new level each day since then and aims to achieve new target regularly with a clear vision and understanding for the future. With the head office in Muscat, it tries to diversify itself in the business field.

There are three sister companies working under the umbrella of The Second national LLC

  • Al Fahud United Reconstruction LLC
  • Al Fahud Trading and Contracting
  • Al Fahud United Engineering LLC

Services

The Company excels in the folowwing services Oil Field Drilling Chemicals & Transportation

Chemicals

The Second National LLC is one of the leading suppliers of drilling chemicals in Oman. We deal with various kinds of chemicals used in drilling wells. We, employ the latest technology and processing techniques to provide a diversified grade of chemicals obtaining to match the specific needs of various industries.

As a business policy, we are fully dedicated to achieving very high levels of customer satisfaction by providing products and services which make us second to none in the global village market of specialty products. Our products, services, and manufacturing are strongly supported by prudent research and development department.

Our quality assurance systems, well-equipped manufacturing equipment and highly trained and motivated industry professionals ensure the timely availability of highly consistent and reliable products and services all around the world at globally competitive market terms.

Transportation

The Second National Company also provide the service of Transportation. It manages the transportation of cargo via road. The major services include:

  • Trucking of Goods
  • Loading and offloading
  • Supply of 40FT Flatbed Trailer

The steps that we follow as a part of our processes are given below:
